Facing pirates and dangers galore, can he survive the journey through space?

Season 1
Gene Starwind and his young partner Jim Hawking, who, after a bodyguard job goes wrong, find themselves the unlikely owners of the advanced spaceship Outlaw Star. Thrust into a galactic adventure, the crew must travel to the legendary Galactic Leyline, facing off against pirates, assassins, and other dangers along the way. The season introduces their growing crew, which includes the android Melfina, the assassin Twilight Suzuka, and the shapeshifting Aisha Clan-Clan, as they work to uncover the ship's mysteries and search for a legendary treasure.
