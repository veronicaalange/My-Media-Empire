Traumatized and downtrodden by horrible accidents that gave them superhuman abilities, but left them scarred for life, a team of unlikely heroes finds purpose investigating the weirdest phenomena in existence—and protecting Earth from what they find. Part support group, part superhero team, the Doom Patrol is a band of superpowered freaks who fight for a world that wants nothing to do with them.
